Reindeer Munch

Description

Easy and fun to make this snack with the kids. Ideal replacement to the cookies for Santa!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups crispy rice cereal squares(such as Rice Chex)
  • 2 cups thin pretzel sticks
  • 1 cup cocktail peanuts
  • 1 cup candy-coated milk chocolate pieces(such as M&M's)
  • 1(12 ounce) package white vanilla baking chips

Steps

  1. Stir cereal squares, pretzel sticks, peanuts, and milk chocolate together in a large bowl.
  2. Melt the vanilla baking chips in a microwave-safe glass or ceramic bowl in 30-second intervals, stirring after each melting, for 1 to 3 minutes (dependig on your microwave). Drizzle melted chips over cereal mixture. Pour mixture out onto a large piece of aluminum foil and cool for 30 minutes. Break into bite-size pieces.
